We Take Care of Your Teeth

Establish since 2005, Gellioya Clinic is a unique dental clinic in Gellioya with team of certified professional dentists who have a passion for science of dental with art of cosmetic dentistry. Our goal is to provide optimal dental health within our state-of-the-art facilities in the relaxing environment. We have served thousands of clients all over the region. Our team of dentists and dental specialists at Gellioya Clinic are experienced and highly qualified, accredited both locally and globally.

We are committed to provide you the highest standards of personalized dental care in a gentle, efficient and professional manner Whether you want to solve a problem or create a new perfect smile, we are confident that we can help.

Gentle and Caring Dental Treatments to Ensure Your Experience is as Comfortable as Possible

We offer a single stop solution for all routine and cosmetic dentistry. With a combination of excellent OMF Surgeons, sophisticated diagnostics, digital X - Ray technology and Dental Surgeons with years of experience, our Clinic offers a personalized, hygienic and affordable dental service.
